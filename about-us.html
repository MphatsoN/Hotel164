About us

More than a Business Travel Destination

Hotel 164 is located less than a kilometer from the gushing Orange River, Upington, also known as the Northern Cape’s “river city.” As the closest major center to the Kgalagadi Transfrontier Park, the town is seen as the gateway to the Kalahari Desert, a region home to the Khoi, San, and Nama people. The town truly epitomizes the Northern Cape’s tagline of “the Province of Extremes, with the sunniest location on the planet and the longest aircraft runway in Africa. Orange River Wine Cellars is the 2nd largest in the world and Hotel164 is only 250 meters away from it.
